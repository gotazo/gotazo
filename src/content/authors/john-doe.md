The Gotazo editorial team focuses on researching and reviewing furniture designed for small apartments and compact homes.

Our content is built around one goal: helping people choose furniture that fits comfortably in **1BHK and small 2BHK apartments**.

We analyze product dimensions, space efficiency, build quality, and practicality before recommending any item. Our guides focus on:

- Compact sofas for small living rooms  
- Sofa cum beds for multipurpose spaces  
- Space-saving coffee tables and dining tables  
- Storage beds and practical bedroom furniture  

All recommendations are based on careful research to help readers **avoid furniture that overcrowds small homes** and instead choose pieces that improve comfort and functionality.

Our mission is simple: **make small apartments more livable with smarter furniture choices.**
