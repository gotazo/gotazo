## How to Choose a Sofa for Small Living Rooms

Before buying, check:

- Width between **60–72 inches**
- Depth under **36 inches**
- Doorway clearance
- Walking space after placement
- Avoid bulky recliners or L-shapes

👉 In small apartments, **depth matters more than width**.
