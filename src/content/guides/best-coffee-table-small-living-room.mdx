## How to Choose a Coffee Table for Small Living Rooms

Before buying, keep these points in mind:

- **Width:** Under 80 cm  
- **Depth:** 30–45 cm  
- **Height:** Should match sofa seat height  
- **Design:** Round or nesting tables save space  
- **Storage:** 2-tier or drawer options are useful  

👉 Avoid bulky rectangular tables in tight spaces.
