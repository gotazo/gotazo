# Quick Buying Checklist for Small Living Rooms

Before choosing a sofa, keep these practical guidelines in mind:

- Ideal width: **60–72 inches**
- Ideal depth: **28–36 inches**
- Measure your wall length before buying
- Ensure the sofa fits through your doorways
- Avoid bulky **L-shaped or recliner models**

For small apartments, **depth matters more than most people realize.**
